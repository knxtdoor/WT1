About Shakespeare Co.
  • Who are we?
    Shakespeare Co. is an young beauty product company that brings innovative solutions to the field of beauty preservation.
  • What do we do?
    Here at Shakespeare Co., we have one goal: preserve your beauty. Beauty does not last forever on its own, and we think that that is the greatest tragedy of life. Everyone should strive to maintain their beauty, as well as seek out ways to continue their beauty beyond their life. Shakespeare Co. frames its products around this fact, and offers two different methods of eternal beauty preservation. Each method has its pros and cons, but both are guaranteed to make you beautiful forever.
  • Why are we better?
    Other Beauty companies tout the power of their products, citing the fact that they will last for hours or days. These products merely subdue the constant attack of time, while ours soundly defeat it. Our products are even resilient against the power of Nature, another force that seeks to destroy your beauty. Additionally, our products use all natural ingredients such as sound devices, symbolism, and imagery.
Method 1: Procreation
The first way to maintain your beauty is to procreate. As some of you may know, children live and carry on your family name and preserve your genes. Having a child will bestow your beauty onto them, extending its reach. Your child will have some of your beautiful features, and give you an excuse when someone asks what happened to you in old age. Your ultimate goal should be to find a suitable husband or wife and make a child, directly preserving your beauty and lineage forever.
  • Step 1: Fall in love with someone.
  • Step 2: Marry this person.
  • Step 3: Have a child.
These 3 simple steps will allow your beauty to last forever.
Procreation is also critical in maintaining the appearance of you and your family. Failing to have children can marr the reputation of your house, leaving a dark mark under your name on the family tree. When you are old and frail, and somebody asks you where your beauty lies, having to respond with "my deep sunken eyes" is one of the most humiliating things possible.
Method 2: Metaphorical
The second method of beauty preservation is what we call “Metaphorical”. The main idea of this method is to create a work of art that totally encapsulates your beauty. The most common form of art that is used is poetry. The key is to create a perfect description of yourself using only poetic devices and language, and then share this poem with as many people as possible. If done correctly, you will become a famous poet and your work will last forever, therefore preserving your beauty. This method has the potential to be much more effective than method 1, however it is also quite difficult to achieve, as writing the perfect poem(s) is never simple.
  • Step 1: Create a work of art, preferably a poem, that captures your beauty.
  • Step 2: Publish and share your perfect art.
  • Step 3: Become famous, thereby preserving your likeness within the art.
These steps are the most straightforward way to extend the lifespan of your beauty to eternity.
